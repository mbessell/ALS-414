About Message Board.

The purpose of the message board is to be able to connect with fellow PCOS patients. This platform allows PCOS patients to post about, as well as read about experiences navigating PCOS. This message board allows members to be involved in a community with whatever extent of anonymity they are comfortable with. The benefits of having access to stories from patients navigating PCOS include a sense of community and can combat feelings of isolation when going about the trying experiences that PCOS can cause.
